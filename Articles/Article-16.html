Plant Beauty, Value, and Legacy: Trees for Your Farm with Greensiri

At Greensiri, your farmland isn’t just about food — it’s about building a legacy. Along with vegetables, fruits, and healing herbs, we offer thoughtfully selected ornamental, timber, and high-value tree species. These trees elevate your land — aesthetically, ecologically, and economically.
Whether you want to add elegance to your weekend retreat, diversify long-term value, or support biodiversity — we’ve got you covered.

Ornamental Trees: Where Nature Becomes Art

These trees and plants turn your land into a lush, vibrant paradise — loved by birds, bees, and every visitor who steps in.

🌳 Ficus benjamina (Weeping Fig)

Elegant, air-purifying, and sculptural — perfect for shade and boundary landscaping.

🌿 Terminalia

Is a hardy, fast-growing tree often used for roadside and landscape beautification. Adds structure and symmetry to any property.

🌴 Bird of Paradise Palm

A showstopper with tropical flair — wide leaves and bright flowers that resemble birds in flight.

🍃 Calathea lutea

Bold-leaved ornamental with a lush, tropical look — adds depth and contrast to garden paths or shaded corners.

🌸 Plumeria (Frangipani) Varieties

Fragrant, colorful, and symbolic — used in spiritual and decorative gardens. Low maintenance and deeply rewarding.

🌺 Geranium Varieties

Vibrant flowers with aromatic leaves. Ideal for borders and colorful beds.

🌸 Hibiscus

Great for pollinators, rich in color, and used for natural remedies. Flowers attract butterflies and brighten your space.

🌹 Rose

The timeless bloom. Symbol of love and beauty — with varieties to suit every mood and climate.

🌼 Jasmine

Highly fragrant and culturally significant. Perfect near homes, patios, or along walking paths.

🌲 Timber Trees: Long-Term Green Wealth

Looking for future income or a retirement cushion? Timber trees grow with you — slowly but steadily increasing your land’s value.

🌳 Teak (Sagwan)

India’s most prized timber. Long lifespan, high durability, and top demand in furniture and construction.

🌿 Mahogany

Known for its rich reddish-brown wood, used in premium interiors. An ideal legacy crop.

🌲 Silver Oak

Fast-growing and multipurpose. Used for plywood, shade, and even as a support tree in coffee plantations.

🌳 Neem

A sacred, medicinal tree that’s also pest-repellent. It provides shade, protection, and is ideal for organic farms.

💎 High-Value Trees: The Premium Picks

Invest in nature’s gold. These trees take time — but their yield is unmatched in value, uniqueness, and demand.

🪵 Red Sandalwood (Raktha Chandanam)

Rare, exotic, and high-value — used in cosmetics, medicines, and crafts. Protected and precious.

🌲 Sandalwood (Srigandha)

An evergreen tree prized for its fragrant heartwood and essential oil. Legally regulated, and highly lucrative.
