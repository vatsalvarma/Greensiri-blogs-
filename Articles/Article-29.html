Types of Houses for Farmland Living

Farmland properties offer a peaceful escape from city life. Whether you dream of an eco-friendly retreat, a luxurious villa, or a cozy cabin — here's your complete guide to the best farmland home types in India.

Peaceful Living Options for Every Lifestyle

Compare 10 house types by style, suitability, and budget — find your perfect match.

1. Eco-Friendly Homes 🌿

Sustainable homes built with green materials, designed to minimize environmental impact while offering modern comfort.

Best For: Environmentally conscious individuals

Price Range: ₹60 lakh – ₹1.5 crore

2. Log Cabin 🪵

Rustic wooden homes that offer a natural, cosy aesthetic with great insulation — perfect for off-grid living.

Best For: Nature lovers, off-grid living

Price Range: ₹40 lakh – ₹1.5 crore

3. Ranch-Style Homes 🌾

Single-story homes with open layouts and large outdoor spaces that blend seamlessly with farmland surroundings.

Best For: Large families, rural living

Price Range: ₹70 lakh – ₹2 crore

4. Cabins 🏕️

Wooden cabins provide a cozy, rustic escape with essential comforts — ideal for a peaceful countryside lifestyle.

Best For: Permanent or weekend homes

Price Range: ₹3,00,000 – ₹25,00,000

5. Courtyard Houses 🏛️

Homes with a central open courtyard that offers privacy, enhanced sunlight, and a strong connection to nature.

Best For: Full-time or luxury homes

Price Range: ₹10,00,000 – ₹50,00,000

6. Tiny Homes 🏠

Compact, efficient homes designed for minimalist, budget-friendly living without sacrificing comfort.

Best For: Singles or couples, simple lifestyle

Price Range: ₹15 lakh – ₹50 lakh

7. Luxury Villa 💎

Spacious, high-end homes with premium features, lavish amenities, and beautifully landscaped surroundings.

Best For: High-income individuals, prestige living

Price Range: ₹1.5 crore – ₹5 crore+

8. Cottage 🏡

Charming, small homes with a cozy rustic feel, perfect for a peaceful countryside retreat or vacation getaway.

Best For: Small families or vacation homes

Price Range: ₹30 lakh – ₹70 lakh

9. Cob Houses 🌍

Eco-friendly homes made from clay, sand, and straw — durable, energy-efficient, and naturally beautiful.

Best For: Eco-friendly & DIY projects

Price Range: ₹3,00,000 – ₹12,00,000

10. Treehouses 🌳

Modern treehouses offer a unique, adventurous living experience with stunning views and deep connection to nature.

Best For: Eco-retreats, adventurous stays

Price Range: ₹5,00,000 – ₹20,00,000

Find Your Perfect Countryside Home

From simple cozy cabins to luxurious villas surrounded by greenery, farmland offers a wide range of housing options for every lifestyle and budget. Whether you're looking for a permanent home, a weekend retreat, or a sustainable investment — there's a farmland home type designed just for you. Choose wisely, live peacefully.
