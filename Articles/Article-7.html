Cultivate Health - Grow Medicinal Herbs & Nutritious Greens at Greensiri Farmlands

At Greensiri Farmlands, we don’t just grow food — we grow wellness, tradition, and sustainability. Alongside seasonal vegetables and fruit trees, we offer you the chance to plant and nurture a special selection of health-boosting plants and greens on your plot — all managed by our expert team.
These plants are more than just crops. They are remedies, rituals, and superfoods. Imagine growing your own aloe vera for skin care, fresh mint for tea, or spinach for your child’s lunchbox — all from your own slice of land.

🌱 Special Purpose Plants: Natural Healers at Your Fingertips

These aren’t just any plants — they’re natural wellness allies with medicinal, aromatic, and everyday practical uses.

🌼 Rosemary

A fragrant herb known for its memory-boosting and antioxidant-rich properties. It’s great for cooking and acts as a natural pest repellent in your garden.

🌵 Aloe Vera

The king of natural healing. From burns to digestion to skincare, aloe vera is a multi-use miracle plant that thrives easily with little water.

🌿 Jade Plant

Often called the “money plant,” this succulent not only adds beauty but is believed to bring good luck and purify air. Easy to grow, ideal for corners of your plot.

🌾 Lemongrass

A refreshing, aromatic grass that’s perfect for tea, oil extraction, and even mosquito repellent. Lemongrass adds beauty and function to any plot.

🥬 Asparagus

A nutrient-dense vegetable packed with vitamins and minerals. It’s a hardy perennial — meaning it keeps giving year after year.

🍃 Maruvam (Marjoram or Dried Oregano)

An ancient herb known in Ayurveda for treating colds and digestive issues. Adds flavor to food and value to your wellness patch.

🥗 Everyday Greens: Nutritious, Easy-to-Grow & Loved by All

Fresh, fast-growing, and deeply nourishing — these greens are must-haves in every health-conscious household. And growing them yourself ensures zero pesticides, full flavor, and maximum freshness.

🌿 Coriander (Dhaniya)

A staple in every Indian kitchen, coriander is a fast-growing herb used in everything from chutneys to garnishes. Plus, its seeds can be harvested too!

🌱 Mint (Pudina)

Fragrant, fast-growing, and versatile — great for chutneys, teas, and even cooling drinks in summer.

🥬 Spinach (Palak)

Loaded with iron and perfect for every age group. Spinach grows quickly and loves partial shade.

🍀 Fenugreek (Methi)

An easy-to-grow plant whose leaves and seeds are both edible and highly nutritious. Excellent for hair, skin, and diabetes support.

🍃 Amaranthus (Chaulai)

A forgotten supergreen — rich in calcium, iron, and protein. Easy to grow and delicious in stir-fries or curries.
