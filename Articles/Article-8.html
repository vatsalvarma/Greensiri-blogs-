Cultivate Life - Grow Your Own Farm Fresh Food at Greensiri Tarini Farms

In today’s fast-paced, city-driven lives, we often forget the joy of growing what we eat. At Greensiri Tarini Farms, we bring you back to the roots — literally. Nestled near the serene hills of Lepakshi, just a short drive from Bangalore, our managed farmlands offer not just a plot of land but a way of life. Here, you can handpick what you grow — from juicy tomatoes to exotic dragon fruits — and we take care of the rest.
Whether you’re dreaming of weekend retreats or investing in long-term, sustainable income, Greensiri gives you the soil, support, and soul to make it happen.

A Garden of Greens: Choose from 19 Home-Grown Vegetables 🥕

Imagine stepping into your own plot and harvesting vegetables straight from the earth — free from chemicals, full of life. At Greensiri, we offer a wide variety of crops tailored to the local climate and soil, ensuring abundant yields with minimal effort.
Here’s what you can grow:
• Tomato, Chilli, Capsicum – The vibrant trio that adds flavor and color to every meal.
• Carrot, Radish (Mooli) – Crunchy roots, rich in nutrients, perfect for juicing or raw munching.
• Spring Onion, Potato – Everyday staples that are surprisingly rewarding to grow.
• Ginger, Turmeric – Grow your own healing roots, prized for their medicinal value.
• Cauliflower – A cool-season crop loved by health-conscious families.
• Okra (Bhindi) – Easy to grow and highly productive, perfect for Indian kitchens.
• Bottle Gourd (Lauki), Sponge Gourd (Turai), Bitter Gourd (Karela) – Light, hydrating vegetables ideal for a detox diet.
• Pumpkin (Kaddu) – From traditional dishes to festive sweets, pumpkin is a joy to grow.
• Cucumber (Kheera) – Perfect for the summer, easy to grow even for beginners.
• Chow Chow, Brinjal – Unique flavors to add variety to your farm menu.
• Peas (Matar) – Sweet, seasonal, and delightfully fresh from the pod.

🍇 The Fruitful Side of Life: 26 Fruit Crops to Enrich Your Orchard

While veggies bring quick satisfaction, fruit trees are the legacy crops. Once planted, they yield for years, bringing beauty, nutrition, and long-term value to your land.
At Greensiri, you can choose from a wide palette of fruit-bearing trees and seasonal fruits:

🍏 Classic & Exotic Fruit Trees

• Jackfruit (Dwarf & Big) – High-yielding and space-efficient.
• Mango – A timeless favorite and income-generating tree.
• Guava, Sapota (Chikoo) – Hardy trees, great for juice and snacking.
• Banana, Papaya – Fast-growing tropical staples.
• Lemon, Orange, Sweet Lime – Citrus trees that keep your immunity boosted year-round.
• Avocado – The green gold of modern nutrition.
• Custard Apple, Ram Phal, Lakshman Phal – Sweet, rare fruits that stand out in any orchard.

🍓 Seasonal Fruits & Berries

• Dragon Fruit – Striking, drought-resistant, and highly marketable.
• Muskmelon, Watermelon – Summer sweetness is at its best.
• Strawberry, Berries – Perfect for hobby growers and local markets.
• Jamun, Water Apple – Native fruits with cooling and medicinal benefits.
• Grapes, Fig, Date Palm – A touch of the Mediterranean in South India.

🌾 Bonus Seasonal Crops

• Sugarcane – Ideal for jaggery or juice production.
• Corn – A satisfying crop that’s easy to grow and fun to harvest.
